Why BestofTweeps?

A few years back I was joined twitter.com and found it the best place to be than any other social media sites. here what makes unique is the quality of the people I follow. I'm here for over the years but to my surprise still, there are lots of the high-quality twitter handles who used to post interestingly in Tamil. So we want to make it the easiest process to follow the high-quality Tamil handles with this one-stop curated platform.

About BestofTweeps

Dear Tweeps.,

BestofTweeps is a gender-neutral living directory of contentful people. Our mission is to provide a space to find outstanding people to follow, look for a mentor, make new friends, or discover talented fun/interesting/experienced/knowledgeable Tamil people to learn from them.

Request an Edit or Opt Out
